About US

FAIMA MAHARASHTRA - FOUNDATION OF ALL INTERNAL MALAYALEE ASSOCIATION

AIMA MAHARASHTRA (FOUNDATION OF IALL NTERNAL MALAYALEE ASSOCIATION.Society Registration No.Thane/0000029/2024, NITI AYOG Darpan Registration No.MH/2024/0404800 )is the Maharashtra Malayalee's Umbrella organization and Affiliated with Federation of All India Marunadan Malayalee Associations (FAIMA formed In 1995) is a socio-cultural National organization which represents more than five hundred registered, non-political and non-religious, Malayalee Associations functioning outside Kerala but within the Indian Territory.

The main objects of the" FAIMA MAHARASHTRA " are to foster unity and brotherhood among the various Malayalee associations functioning in Maharashtra, to organize various activities and programmes, to promote unity and cultural heritage of God's own country and Maharashtra. To achieve the above objects, the association has taken up so many "Herculean Tasks" for the welfare of the Non-resident Malayalee community with in India in particular, and the whole society in general. We succeeded in many of them, which brought fame and strength to the organization. We still strive hard to find way to address so many burning problems of our society irrespective of the caste, creed and language. FAIMA MAHARASHTRA coordinate and provides Social, Cultural, Educational, Artistic, Athletic Charity and activities including Linguistic support to the Malayali diaspora in the Maharashtra state. FAIMA MAHARASHTRA strives to nourish the traditional values of people of Kerala Origin and help with the assimilation into the Great Maharashtrian culture.

FAIMA Maharashtra unites and empowers the Malayalee community in Maharashtra, preserving cultural heritage and fostering brotherhood. Our mission is to address societal challenges and uplift the community, embracing the values of both Kerala and Maharashtra.

The vision and commitment of FAIMA MAHARASHTRA which believes in community welfare is to ensure that the trust has the facilities to extend a helping hand to those who require it. It is our mission to deliver the needs of the community irrespective of caste, creed or economic status. We have 75000 volunteers serving from all ages and backgrounds in india. In MAHARASHTRA all 36 district 25000 volunteers serving from all ages and backgrounds.

In the recent past, we have rendered help and assistance to people during flood at Chiplun, Mohad, Roha, Kolhapur, Sangli and our volunteers rendered relentless service during Covid Pendamic through out the state.

FAIMA Maharashtra is a socio-cultural organization uniting and empowering the Malayalee community across Maharashtra through welfare, cultural, and educational initiatives.
